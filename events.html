Big Smoke Evenings

Friday, November 13
Saturday, November 14

$275 per ticket per night

Friday and Saturday evenings, from 6:30-9:30 p.m., are our traditional,fun-filled, Big Smoke eve­­nings. At these Big Smoke evenings you can join your fellow cigar lovers in an atmosphere of good cigars, good drinks, good food and great companionship.

As at every Big Smoke, you have the opportunity to walk around and sample cigars from more than 25 premium cigar producers that will be there. Your ticket price includes a cigar from each of the cigar exhibitors.

Included in your ticket price are premium liquors, wines, beer, and other fine spirits from the extensive array of drink exhibitors and a delicious buffet specially created for a cigar maven. Also on display are luxury cars, golf equipment and many more items appealing to our aficionados.
